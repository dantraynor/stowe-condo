Stowe, Vermont

Nestled in the heart of Vermont's Green Mountains, Stowe is a charming New England town known for its spectacular natural beauty and year-round recreational opportunities. With a perfect blend of outdoor adventure and small-town charm, Stowe offers visitors an authentic Vermont experience.

Often called the "Ski Capital of the East," Stowe is home to Vermont's highest peak, Mount Mansfield, and offers some of the best skiing and snowboarding in the Northeast. But Stowe is much more than just a winter destination - with vibrant fall foliage, refreshing summer activities, and the beauty of spring renewal, it's truly a four-season resort town.

The town features a historic village center with a classic white-steepled church, covered bridges, and a variety of shops, galleries, and restaurants that showcase Vermont's local products and craftsmanship.

Local Transportation

Getting Around Stowe

By Car

  • Having your own vehicle is recommended for exploring the Stowe area.
  • Most attractions are within a 5-15 minute drive from our condo.
  • Parking is generally free and plentiful throughout the area.
  • Winter driving conditions can be challenging - all-wheel drive is recommended from November through April.

Mountain Road Shuttle

  • Free shuttle service along Mountain Road (Route 108).
  • Operates daily during ski season (November-April).
  • Limited summer service (weekends only, June-October).
  • Stops at major resorts, restaurants, and shopping areas.
  • The nearest shuttle stop is 0.3 miles from our condo.

Taxis & Rideshare

  • Several local taxi companies serve the Stowe area.
  • Uber and Lyft have limited availability - more reliable in high season.
  • We recommend saving local taxi numbers in your phone before arrival.
  • Stowe Taxi: (802) 555-1234
  • Green Mountain Cab: (802) 555-5678

Airport Transportation

  • Burlington International Airport (BTV) is the closest major airport, 45 minutes away.
  • Airport shuttle services available with advance reservation.
  • Car rentals available at the airport.
  • For private airport transfers, we recommend Green Mountain Transit: (802) 555-9012

Note: For the best experience in Stowe, we strongly recommend having access to a vehicle, especially if you plan to explore beyond the main village area or visit during off-peak seasons when shuttle service is limited.
